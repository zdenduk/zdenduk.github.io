Obsessed with video games from an early age, I set a goal to become a part of creating them. Since then, I have been learning how to code, studied data structures and mastering design patterns.

I started my professional journey very young, working at CSOB Bank, and have since built a rich portfolio of experience with companies like Alza, CGI, and now Oracle, where I’m part of the ComputeFarm team in Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI).

I have created projects in multiple programming languages - Java, C#, Matlab and completed homework assignments in several others, i.e., Python, C and C++. In these projects, I used both NoSQL and SQL databases, Spring and other largely-used technologies.

I am good at things I am passionate about - I was one of the best players on EUNE server in Teamfight Tactics and Grandmaster in League of Legends. I still keep up somewhat, ranked Master in TFT and Flex Queue. Beyond gaming, I’m passionate about cars—most recently restoring and personalizing my 1988 Mazda RX-7 FC3S.
